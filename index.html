Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does National Academy College offer?

We offer +2 Science, +2 Management, Computer Science and Law programs, each with dedicated faculty and facilities.

When does the admission process begin?

Admissions typically open in Baisakh each year. Visit our Admissions page for exact dates and the full application process.

Does the college provide scholarships?

Yes, merit and need-based scholarships are available. Details are shared during the admission counselling session.

Is hostel or transportation available?

We assist students with verified local hostel options and coordinate shared transport routes around Janakpurdham.

How can I contact the admissions office?

Call 041-591895 / 9812176793, email info@nationalacademyhss.edu.np, or use the contact form below.
