Admissions FAQ

Common Questions

Is there an entrance test?

Science and Computer Science require a basic aptitude assessment; Management and Law are counselling-based admission.

Can I change my program after enrolling?

Program changes are possible within the first two weeks of the session, subject to seat availability.

What is the fee payment schedule?

Admission fees are paid at enrollment; monthly fees are collected on the first week of each month.
