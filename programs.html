Our Programs

Four structured pathways — each built around real curriculum, lab access and career outcomes.

+2 Programme

Science

Our flagship Science program covers Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics with structured lab practicals, preparing students for medicine, engineering and pure sciences at university level.

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics & English
  • Weekly hands-on laboratory sessions
  • Pathway to MBBS, BSc, Engineering entrance prep

+2 Programme

Management

Build a foundation in Accounting, Economics and Business Studies designed for students headed toward BBA, BBS or professional commerce careers.

  • Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies & Marketing
  • Case-study and project-based learning
  • Pathway to BBA, BBS, Chartered Accountancy
Apply for Management
Diploma / +2

Computer Science

A practical, project-driven program covering programming fundamentals, networking and applied computing — built for the region's growing digital economy.

  • Programming fundamentals, web basics & networking
  • Dedicated computer lab with modern workstations
  • Pathway to BSc.CSIT, BIT and IT careers
Apply for Computer Science
Bachelor Programme

Law

Our Law program builds foundational legal knowledge and practical advocacy skills through moot-court style sessions, preparing graduates for the Bar and public service.

  • Constitutional, criminal & civil law foundations
  • Moot court and practical advocacy training
  • Pathway to Bar Council registration and legal practice
