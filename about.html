About National Academy College

Rooted in Janakpurdham, built for the ambitions of Dhanusha's students.

Our Story

Nearly Two Decades of Academic Commitment

National Academy College was founded in Balmikinagar, Janakpurdham with a clear purpose: to give students across Dhanusha access to disciplined, high-quality higher secondary education without having to leave the region.

What began as a small campus offering Science and Management has grown into a multi-program institution — now also home to Computer Science and Law — while keeping the same close, mentorship-driven approach to teaching.
